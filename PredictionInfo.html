🎯 JSSA Prediction Contest

Think you know which team is stacked? Review the rosters, make your picks, and see how your prediction skills stack up against the rest of the league.

How It Works
1
Check the Game Day rosters

When the Game Day Teams sheet is posted to the JSSA website, review the rosters for each field. That's your scouting report.

2
Make your picks before 9:00 AM

The prediction ballot opens when rosters are published and closes at 9:00 AM on game day. Click the prediction link in the Game Day Teams sheet to submit your picks.

3
Pick one game or all of them

You don't have to predict every game — pick as many or as few as you like. Only your most recent submission for each game counts, so you can always change your mind before 9:00 AM.

4
Watch the live odds shift

As members submit their picks, the live odds on the JSSA website update in real time — you can see where the crowd is leaning right up until the ballot closes.

5
Results are reported after the games

Game results are texted to 412-721-0173 by division board members or participating players after each game. Only the winning side — Home (H) or Visitor (V) — needs to be reported.

Leaderboard & Standings
How rankings work

Your accuracy is calculated as correct picks ÷ total picks. The leaderboard is sorted by accuracy, with ties broken by total correct predictions.

Qualification rule

You must submit at least 4 picks in a month to qualify for that month's standings. Players with fewer than 4 picks are not ranked.

When standings update

The leaderboard updates automatically after each game day's results are entered. Check back after game day to see the latest standings.
