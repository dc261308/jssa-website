Community Night · Tuesday, July 7

Free scoop at Village Scoop Shack

Thanks to the generosity of an anonymous donor from the Jupiter Senior Softball League, all JSSA members and their families are invited to enjoy a free scoop of ice cream on Tuesday, July 7 at Village Scoop Shack. It's part of their ongoing Tunnel to Towers Tuesday — 10% of all sales support military members and first responders across the country.

Visit Village Scoop Shack →

About the JSSA

More than a game

The JSSA plays softball in Jupiter, Florida three days a week, every week throughout the year. We play hard, we play fair, and we build a brotherhood that goes well beyond the field. All players 55 and over are welcome.
