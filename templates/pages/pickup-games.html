JSSA pickup games are organized every Monday, Wednesday and Friday from April through December. Members who want to play must sign up in advance of each game day.

How sign-ups work

  • Teams for all games are made in advance of game day. Players must sign up by 3:00 PM the day before each game to be added to a team roster.
  • To sign up, all players must be registered JSSA members and use the JSSA Player Schedule Assistant to manage their own game schedule. By entering your name and unique player code, you can access every game on the schedule and add or remove yourself at any time, from your phone or computer.
  • At 3:00 PM the day before games, the JSSA board restricts access to the game schedule to configure all games, teams and fields.
  • Scheduled players receive an email the evening before each game confirming their roster spot and team for the next day.

Save these links

Bookmark these on your phone, iPad or computer so they're always handy:

  • JSSA Schedule Assistant — add or remove yourself from games
  • JSSA Game Schedule — see the full schedule

Need help using the Schedule Assistant? Email jssagames@gmail.com.
